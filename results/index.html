P&D
Priya & David M.
Miami, FL · Edgewater Waterfront Condo · New Host, 4 months listed
The Situation
Priya and David listed their 3BR Edgewater condo two weeks before Art Basel: Miami's single highest-demand week of the year, when competitors in their category charge $380–$480/night. They were charging $145 flat. With only 8 reviews and a page 12 search position, the listing was algorithmically invisible. Monthly revenue: $2,061.
The Outcome
55 days after audit completion: $248 dynamic base rate, $480 during Art Basel, 82% occupancy, 4.93★ rating, #6 in market. Monthly revenue: $5,594. The Art Basel week alone: priced at $480/night for 7 nights: generated $3,360. Marketics' annual management fee for this property: $6,712. The first Art Basel week covered 50% of it.
“We had no idea what we were sitting on. Marketics repriced us from $145 flat to $480 for Art Basel week, and we sold out. That one week covered half our first year of management fees. Now we understand why market intelligence actually matters.”
— Priya M., Miami, FL · Result documented at day 90
01
Audit: Days 1–7
Full competitive landscape mapped
Identified 12 comparable listings in Edgewater pricing $380–$480 during Art Basel. Confirmed listing photos were hotel-angled instead of view-first. Title missing "waterfront" and "Edgewater": the two highest-converting keywords in the market.
↳ $335 avg price gap identified vs comp set
02
Architecture: Days 8–14
Listing rebuilt for search + conversion
Title rewritten: "Edgewater Waterfront · Biscayne Bay Views · Walk to Wynwood." Lead photo switched to skyline-at-dusk shot. Description restructured to front-load Art Basel proximity and Wynwood walkability. House manual created for 5-star operational consistency.
↳ Click-through rate increased within 72hrs of relaunch
03
Pricing: Days 14–20
Dynamic model activated, Art Basel priced
Base rate lifted to $190 weekdays, $248 weekends. Art Basel window (Dec 4–11): priced at $480/night: matching top-10 comparable listings. Midweek fill floor set at $155 to accelerate review velocity entering the high season.
↳ Art Basel 7-night block: $3,360 revenue
04
Reviews: Days 14–45
29 new reviews in 45 days
Midweek pricing at $155 filled shoulder nights and generated the review burst needed to move the algorithm. By day 45: 29 new reviews, 4.93★ average, listing moved from page 12 to top-20 in Miami. Ultra Music Festival window priced next.
↳ Page 12 → #6 in Market by Day 55
05
Compound: Days 55–90
Flywheel locked in, Ultra priced
Ultra Music Festival (March) priced at $390/night. With #6 ranking, organic bookings accelerated. Monthly revenue stabilized at $5,594. 90-day total incremental revenue over pre-Marketics baseline: $10,599.
↳ +$10,599 incremental in first 90 days
