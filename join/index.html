Marketics Co-Host Agreement

This Co-Host Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Marketics, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Marketics"), and the host ("Host") submitting this form. By accepting this Agreement, both parties agree to the following terms.

1. Services

Marketics will provide short-term rental optimization services as an Airbnb co-host, including but not limited to: dynamic pricing strategy, listing optimization, calendar management, review velocity systems, and market intelligence. Services commence within 7 business days of completed onboarding.

2. Compensation

Host agrees to pay Marketics a service fee of 10% of the net payout per booking. Net payout is defined as gross guest charges minus Airbnb's host service fee minus any platform-remitted taxes. Payment is collected automatically via Airbnb's co-host split payment mechanism. There are no additional monthly fees, retainer fees, or setup fees beyond the one-time Onboarding Investment.

3. Onboarding Investment

Host agrees to a one-time $500 USD Onboarding Investment paid at the time of this Agreement. This investment covers the setup, listing audit, strategy development, and implementation work performed in the first 7 days. This investment is fully refundable under the 90-Day Performance Guarantee (Section 6).

4. Co-Host Access

Host agrees to add Marketics as a co-host on their Airbnb account. Co-host access is limited to listing management, pricing, calendar, and guest communication functions within the Airbnb platform. Marketics will not have access to banking information, payout accounts, tax information, or account credentials. Host retains full ownership of the listing and account at all times.

5. Host Obligations

To achieve the performance outcomes described herein, Host agrees to:

  • Implement Marketics' recommendations within 48 hours of receipt
  • Maintain property standards and respond to guest issues promptly
  • Keep the listing active and available for optimization during the term
  • Notify Marketics of any blackout dates, property issues, or platform changes
  • Not make unilateral pricing or listing changes without prior coordination

6. 90-Day Performance Guarantee

If Marketics does not increase Host's gross booking revenue by at least 20% in the first 90 days of active service, Host's month 4 service fee will be waived entirely. Additionally, the $500 Onboarding Investment will be fully refunded within 5 business days. This guarantee applies exclusively to Hosts who have fulfilled all obligations under Section 5. Revenue comparison is measured against the 90-day period immediately preceding the commencement of services.

7. Term and Termination

This Agreement commences upon payment of the Onboarding Investment and continues month-to-month. Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice via email to the addresses on file. Upon termination, Marketics will be removed as co-host within 2 business days of the notice period end. Accrued service fees through the termination date remain payable.

8. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential all proprietary information, including pricing strategies, client data, and operational methodologies disclosed during the term of this Agreement.

9. Limitation of Liability

Marketics' liability under this Agreement is limited to the total fees paid by Host in the preceding 90 days. Marketics is not liable for platform changes, Airbnb policy modifications, force majeure events, or Host non-compliance with recommendations.

10. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Florida. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Miami, Florida.

11. Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with Marketics' Privacy Policy and Terms of Service available at marketics.io/legal, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations or representations.
