FAQ

Common Questions
From Hosts.

What does Marketics actually do for my Airbnb?+

Marketics provides fully managed revenue optimization for short-term rentals. That includes dynamic pricing calibrated to your specific market and events, listing copy and photography optimization, Airbnb search ranking strategy, automated guest communication, and ongoing performance monitoring across 79 signals. You stay the host — we handle the revenue science.

How much does Marketics cost?+

Marketics charges 10% of net booking revenue per property. There is a one-time $500 onboarding fee (refundable under the 90-day guarantee). No monthly retainers, no long-term contracts, no hidden fees. We only earn when you earn — if your revenue doesn't increase, we don't get paid more.

What is the 90-day guarantee?+

If your property does not show measurable revenue improvement within 90 days of audit completion, your $500 setup fee is refunded in full. Measurable improvement is defined as a documented increase in monthly revenue compared to your pre-Marketics 90-day baseline. No fine print.

What markets does Marketics operate in?+

Marketics currently operates across 9 active markets in the United States, Canada, and Mexico — including Austin, Miami, Nashville, Scottsdale, Cancún, Tulum, San Juan, San Antonio, and New Orleans. We also serve Muskoka (Ontario) and are expanding to Whistler and additional North American markets through 2026–27. If your market isn't listed, request a free audit — we may already have data on your area.

How quickly will I see results?+

The median time from audit completion to first measurable revenue lift is 72 days across all Marketics-managed properties. Most hosts see initial pricing and ranking improvements within 2–4 weeks. The full optimization — including algorithm positioning, review velocity, and seasonal calibration — compounds over the first 90 days, which is why we benchmark against that window.

Does Marketics work with VRBO and direct bookings, or just Airbnb?+

Marketics optimizes across all major booking platforms including Airbnb, VRBO, and direct booking channels. Our dynamic pricing, guest communication, and listing optimization strategies are platform-aware — meaning we calibrate differently for each channel based on its algorithm, guest behavior, and competitive dynamics.
