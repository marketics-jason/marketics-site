Data Sources
What Powers the Benchmarks

Market benchmark rates and occupancy figures are derived from Marketics' active managed property data across 22 markets, cross-referenced with booking channel API data and wholesale industry data sources and STR market reports updated quarterly.

The "With Marketics" column reflects the actual average performance of Marketics-managed properties in each market, not a theoretical ceiling. These numbers come from properties that went through the full Method.

Assumptions & Limits
What This Calculator Assumes
Monthly revenue uses a 30.4-night month average (365 ÷ 12)
Benchmark data reflects same property category performance: rates vary meaningfully by bedroom count and location within a market
Canadian rates displayed in CAD at ~1.36 exchange rate; Mexican rates in MXN at ~17.5 rate — actual rates fluctuate
The calculator does not account for platform fees, cleaning fees, or operating costs. It measures gross booking revenue only
Marketics figures assume full Method implementation. Results vary by property condition, host responsiveness, and market timing
This tool is provided for estimation purposes only. Individual property performance depends on factors including but not limited to: specific location within a market, property condition and photography quality, listing history and existing review count, host response time, and seasonal timing of onboarding. The revenue gap shown is a statistical estimate based on market averages, not a guarantee of outcome. Marketics' 90-day guarantee uses actual documented metrics, not calculator projections.
